Look at this heavy ball held high up on a shelf. Does it have any energy right now? Even though it is completely still, watch what happens when the shelf vanishes. That sudden burst of motion proved the energy was there the whole time, just waiting. This stored, ready to use power is what we call potential energy. It's easy to think the word potential just means pretend, or that it's future energy that doesn't actually exist yet. But potential energy is entirely real, and it is actively stored inside the object right now. It has that energy purely because of its physical position, fighting against gravity. It's the exact same principle with a drawn bow. The moment you pull that string back, you are packing the weapon with real physical energy. Whether it's a ball raised in the air or a string pulled tight under tension, that stored energy is actively waiting, and the millisecond you let go, it explodes into motion. [BLANK_AUDIO]
